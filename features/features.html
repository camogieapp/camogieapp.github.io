Camogie App Features

New to the Camogie App? Take a peek at our main features below.


With our fully customizable mobile application, your favourite teams will only be a tap away. Make your choice among our wide range of colour schemes and edit the layout of your interface. A series of checkboxes enables you to show or hide all the available content.

Turn on push notifications to never miss a game. We will let you know when tickets for upcoming events are on sale or when the results are out. Of course, it is up to you to opt in or out of this feature. If you wish, you can also turn the notifications off temporarily, for a few days or a few weeks, up to two months.

Like the rest of the app, the news feed is fully customizable. From fixture recaps to interviews, from county news to the latest awards, we combine all your favourite sources in one interface.

Ever thought about connecting with other Camogie fans in your area? In our app, you can join a local group or create a new one. A wide range of settings enables you to share as much or as little information as you want.


Sign up now and download the Camogie App to follow the journey of your favourite players.

And check out our blog to learn everything there is to know about the game.
